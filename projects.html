My Projects

Clear Wave Company

Clear Wave is an initiative that encourages people to participate in collecting ocean or coastal trash by offering financial incentives as a reward for their efforts. Depending on the amount and type of waste removed, people get cash in return. At the end of the day, the waste is collected and sold to recycling companies.

With this website we wanted to convey the company's message in a way that would have more impact on society. Therefore, statistical data, evidence of environmental impact, and photographs and videos were included to increase public awareness.

ShopSmart Company

Shop Smart simplifies grocery shopping in Barcelona by helping people find the best prices for their items across multiple supermarkets, plan their nutrition, and navigate stores efficiently with ShopSmart supermarket maps.

In this project I worked on both the development and creation of the website and the application for different devices including mobile phones, tablets or iPads and computers.

The website emphasizes the people's problem and proposes a clear and concise solution. The company's services are displayed in a user-friendly way so that it is easy to navigate, understand and buy.

The immersive world of Avatar

Theat.er is a theater company from Madrid which performs plays based on films, books or musicals in different places in Spain.

In this specific case, they contacted me to design the website for online ticket sales for their Avatar play. On this website there was information about the group, the sale of tickets and the sale of company merchandising.
