About Me

Hello! I am Mar Grino Sas, a third-year student at Pace University in New York. My work consists of designing and creating websites for different companies in order to improve the company's image and aesthetic, facilitating the search for products, services and information for users and visitors and in this way, exponentially increase sales.

I am constantly improving my work techniques and creativity to offer the best possible service.

Since I was little I have loved everything to be seen in order and harmony so I transferred my passion to technology and, over the years, I can say that I have created works of art and I have been able to help several small companies find their place in today's technology and society.
